Monitoring Children

Monitor children means to watch and check what they do online and offline. It helps to keep them safe.

Check their page and read comments

Check your child’s page and read the comments from time to time and see what you find. Cyberbullying often involves mean-spirited comments.

Block Cyberbullies

Make sure to check your child’s profile and block the bully’s username, email address, or contact information.

Trust is a key factor

Inform your child about what you are doing, otherwise if they find out by themselves, they may act unexpectedly and start hiding issues to preserve their privacy.
