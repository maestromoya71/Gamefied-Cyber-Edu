    "Be nice online—treat others like you wanna be treated! 🌟 No bullying! 🚫😠 Be kind & respectful! 😊💖 Keep secrets safe—no sharing personal stuff! 🤫🔒 And always THINK before you post! 🤔✨ Spread smiles, not oopsies! 😁🌈"

    "Be smart with online friends! 🧠💻 Not everyone is who they say they are… 😶‍🌫️✨ Bad people might act super nice, ‘help’ you, or send gifts 🎁🚩—but it’s TRICKY! 🎣 🚨 Only add people you know in real life! 🚨 Or else… cyberstalking 👀, grooming 🐺, bullying 😢, hacking 🔓, or worse could happen! 😱 Stay safe, cool kids! 👑🔒" Would you like it even simpler or more playful? 😊

    "Watch out for phishing! 🎣🐠 Some sneaky people online try to trick you into sharing secrets—like passwords or info! 🕵️‍♂️🔑 Never click weird links ❌ or reply to strange messages. 📩🚫 If something feels fishy 🐟, ask a grown-up for help! 👩💼👨💼 Stay safe, smarty! 🧠✨".
